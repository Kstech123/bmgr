ABOUT

Dr. Bhushan Magar Sir was born in a village with a farming background. His father was an engineer in the Water Resources Department of the Government of Maharashtra. His school life was happening in places like Pachora. Also his college education was done at Jayhind College in Dhule. After completing their medical education from Maratha Vidya Prasarak Mandal, Nashik, They started there medical service as a Medical Officer from Niphad, Dist. Nashik.

Dr. Bhushan Magar set up Vighnaharta Multispeciality Hospital in Pachora to provide better medical services in the city. Many facilities are under one roof and poor patients are being served free of cost through various government schemes. Also Dr. Bhushan Magar Sir is interested in social, educational and sports fields along with health services, in the year 2013, He established Bhushan Magar (Patil) Foundation, thousands of people are being helped selflessly through this organization.

Dr. Bhushan Magar's popularity and fragrance of medical service has spread everywhere. The idea of doing something different for the society and the patients is always on their minds. He is of the view that an independent medical college should be set up in pachora-bhadgaon area in future and an independent cancer hospital should be set up to eradicate cancer.
